## Overview

Game of Thrones features a vast ensemble of complex characters. Here are the most significant players in the game.

## House Stark

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Eddard Stark" icon="shield">
    Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. Known for his unwavering honor and sense of duty.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Jon Snow" icon="snowflake">
    Ned's bastard son who joins the Night's Watch. His true parentage becomes central to the story.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Arya Stark" icon="sword">
    The youngest Stark daughter who trains as a Faceless Man assassin after witnessing her family's downfall.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sansa Stark" icon="crown">
    The eldest Stark daughter whose journey from naive girl to political mastermind spans the series.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## House Lannister

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tyrion Lannister" icon="wine-glass">
    The witty, intelligent dwarf who uses his mind to survive in a world that judges him for his stature.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cersei Lannister" icon="chess-queen">
    The ambitious queen whose love for her children drives her to ruthless acts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Jaime Lannister" icon="sword">
    The Kingslayer, torn between his reputation and his desire for redemption.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tywin Lannister" icon="building-columns">
    The powerful patriarch who prioritizes family legacy above all else.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## House Targaryen

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Daenerys Targaryen" icon="dragon">
    The Mother of Dragons who rises from exile to become a conquering queen.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Viserys Targaryen" icon="fire">
    Daenerys's cruel brother who believes the Iron Throne is his birthright.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Character deaths

<Warning>
Game of Thrones is notorious for killing major characters. No one is safe in Westeros.
</Warning>

The series subverts traditional fantasy tropes by eliminating protagonists without warning, keeping audiences in constant suspense about who will survive.
