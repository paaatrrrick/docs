## Overview

Game of Thrones features one of the largest ensemble casts in television history. The characters are complex, morally grey, and their fates are never guaranteed.

## House Targaryen

<Note>
The Targaryens are the former ruling dynasty of Westeros, known for their silver hair, violet eyes, and dragon-riding abilities.
</Note>

### Daenerys Targaryen

The "Mother of Dragons" begins as an exiled princess and rises to become one of the most powerful figures in the world. Her journey takes her from:

1. A timid girl sold into marriage
2. Khaleesi of a Dothraki horde
3. Conqueror of Slaver's Bay
4. Claimant to the Iron Throne

### Viserys Targaryen

The cruel elder brother who sells Daenerys to Khal Drogo in exchange for an army, meeting a fitting end when he threatens his sister.

## Character survival rates

<Warning>
Game of Thrones is notorious for killing major characters. No one is safe.
</Warning>

The show subverts traditional fantasy tropes by eliminating protagonists at unexpected moments, creating constant tension and unpredictability throughout the narrative.
