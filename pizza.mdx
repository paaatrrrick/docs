# Pizza guide

Pizza is a flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients. Creating the perfect pizza requires attention to dough preparation, sauce balance, and cooking technique.

## Ingredients

### Pizza dough
- 500g bread flour
- 325ml warm water
- 7g active dry yeast
- 10g salt
- 30ml olive oil

### Pizza sauce
- 400g crushed tomatoes
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Toppings
- 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
- Fresh basil leaves
- Optional: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers

## Making pizza dough

1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let sit for 5 minutes until foamy
2. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl
3. Add yeast mixture and olive oil to flour
4. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic
5. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and rise for 1-2 hours

## Preparing the sauce

1. Mix crushed tomatoes with minced garlic and oregano
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Let stand at room temperature while dough rises

## Assembly and cooking

1. Preheat oven to 250°C (482°F)
2. Roll dough on floured surface to desired thickness
3. Transfer to pizza stone or baking sheet
4. Spread thin layer of sauce, leaving border for crust
5. Add cheese and toppings
6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden

<Tip>
For best results, use a pizza stone preheated in the oven for at least 30 minutes before baking.
</Tip>

## Tips for perfect pizza

- Use high-quality ingredients for better flavor
- Don't overload with toppings - less is more
- Let dough come to room temperature before rolling
- Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible

Your homemade pizza will have crispy edges, melted cheese, and fresh flavors that rival any pizzeria.
