## Overview

Harry Potter is a beloved fantasy series that follows the journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he discovers his magical heritage and battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. The series has captivated readers and viewers worldwide, becoming one of the most successful franchises in literary history.

## Hogwarts houses

- **Gryffindor** — "Where dwell the brave at heart" — Values courage and bravery
- **Slytherin** — "Those cunning folk use any means" — Values ambition and resourcefulness
- **Ravenclaw** — "Where those of wit and learning" — Values intelligence and wisdom
- **Hufflepuff** — "Where they are just and loyal" — Values hard work and loyalty

## Themes

<Note>
"It does not do to dwell on dreams and forget to live." — Albus Dumbledore
</Note>

The series explores themes of:

- Love and sacrifice
- Good versus evil
- Friendship and loyalty
- The power of choice

## The wizarding world

The Wizarding World exists alongside the Muggle (non-magical) world, hidden through various enchantments and secrecy laws. Key locations include Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Diagon Alley, and the Ministry of Magic.

## Legacy

Harry Potter transformed children's literature and created a global phenomenon spanning books, films, theme parks, and a dedicated fan community that continues to grow decades after the final book's release.
