## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas in 1977. It has become one of the most influential and beloved media franchises in history.

## The Force

The Force is a mystical energy field that binds the galaxy together. It can be harnessed by those sensitive to it.

## Key characters

### Heroes

- **Luke Skywalker** - The hero of the original trilogy who brings balance to the Force
- **Princess Leia** - Rebel leader and Luke's twin sister
- **Han Solo** - Smuggler turned Rebel hero
- **Yoda** - Legendary Jedi Master

### Villains

- **Darth Vader** - The fallen Jedi who became a Sith Lord
- **Emperor Palpatine** - The mastermind behind the Galactic Empire
- **Darth Maul** - Sith apprentice with a double-bladed lightsaber
