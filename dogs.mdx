# Dogs

Dogs are domesticated mammals and popular pets known for their loyalty and companionship.

## Characteristics

Dogs come in various breeds, sizes, and temperaments. They are known for:

- Loyalty to their owners
- Intelligence and trainability
- Social nature
- Diverse breeds with unique traits

## Care

Proper dog care includes:

<Steps>
<Step title="Nutrition">
  Provide balanced, age-appropriate food and fresh water daily.
</Step>

<Step title="Exercise">
  Ensure regular physical activity based on breed and energy level.
</Step>

<Step title="Health">
  Schedule regular veterinary checkups and keep vaccinations current.
</Step>

<Step title="Training">
  Use positive reinforcement to teach basic commands and good behavior.
</Step>
</Steps>
