## Overview

Darth Vader, born Anakin Skywalker, is one of the most iconic figures in the Star Wars saga. Once a promising Jedi Knight, he fell to the dark side and became the enforcer of the Galactic Empire before ultimately finding redemption.

## The fall

Anakin's transformation into Darth Vader was driven by:

- **Fear of loss** — Visions of Padmé's death haunted him
- **Manipulation** — Palpatine exploited his vulnerabilities
- **Attachment** — His inability to let go led to his downfall
- **Pride** — He believed he could control the dark side

## Redemption

Despite decades of serving the Empire, Vader's love for his son Luke ultimately proved stronger than his allegiance to the dark side. In his final act, he destroyed Emperor Palpatine to save Luke, fulfilling the prophecy that he would bring balance to the Force.
