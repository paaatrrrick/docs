## Overview

Darth Vader, born Anakin Skywalker, is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. Once a promising Jedi Knight, he fell to the dark side and became a powerful Sith Lord serving the Galactic Empire.

## Key characteristics

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Birth name | Anakin Skywalker |
| Homeworld | Tatooine |
| Affiliation | Galactic Empire, Sith Order |
| Master | Obi-Wan Kenobi (as Jedi), Darth Sidious (as Sith) |
| Signature weapon | Red lightsaber |

## Notable abilities

- **Force mastery** - Exceptional control over the Force, including telekinesis and precognition
- **Lightsaber combat** - One of the most skilled duelists in the galaxy
- **Mechanical expertise** - Inherited from his time as a podracer and Jedi
