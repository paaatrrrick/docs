## Overview

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the most iconic Jedi Masters in the Star Wars galaxy. Known for his wisdom, combat skills, and unwavering dedication to the Jedi Order, he played a pivotal role in shaping the fate of the galaxy.

## Notable achievements

- Defeated Darth Maul in combat after the death of his master Qui-Gon Jinn
- Served as a General in the Clone Wars
- Trained Anakin Skywalker from childhood to Jedi Knight
- Survived Order 66 and went into hiding on Tatooine
- Watched over Luke Skywalker for nearly two decades
- Sacrificed himself aboard the Death Star to help Luke escape

## Fighting style

Obi-Wan was a master of Form III lightsaber combat, known as Soresu. This defensive style focused on:

- Tight, efficient movements
- Energy conservation
- Waiting for opponents to make mistakes
- Protecting against blaster fire

## Legacy

Obi-Wan Kenobi's influence extended far beyond his own lifetime. His training of Luke Skywalker set in motion the events that would ultimately bring balance to the Force and defeat the Sith.
