## Overview

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the most revered Jedi Masters in the history of the Galactic Republic. Known for his wisdom, combat skills, and unwavering dedication to the Jedi Code, he played a pivotal role in some of the most significant events in galactic history.

## Notable accomplishments

- Defeated Darth Maul in combat as a Padawan
- Served as a General during the Clone Wars
- Trained Anakin Skywalker from childhood to Jedi Knight
- Discovered the clone army on Kamino
- Watched over Luke Skywalker on Tatooine for nearly two decades

## Fighting style

Obi-Wan mastered **Form III: Soresu**, the most defensive lightsaber combat form. This style emphasizes:

- Tight, efficient movements
- Strong defensive positions
- Patience and endurance
- Waiting for opponents to make mistakes

## Legacy

Obi-Wan's influence extended far beyond his own lifetime. His training of Luke Skywalker ultimately led to the redemption of Anakin and the fall of the Empire. He exemplified the best qualities of the Jedi Order: selflessness, wisdom, and hope.
