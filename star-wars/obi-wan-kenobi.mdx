## Overview

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the most legendary Jedi Masters in galactic history. Known for his wisdom, diplomatic skills, and mastery of defensive lightsaber combat, he played a pivotal role in the Clone Wars and the training of Anakin Skywalker.

## Combat style

Obi-Wan mastered **Form III: Soresu**, the most defensive lightsaber form. This style focuses on:

- Tight blade work and minimal movements
- Outlasting opponents through superior defense
- Waiting for the perfect counter-attack opportunity

## Legacy

Obi-Wan's influence extended beyond his death. As a Force ghost, he continued to guide Luke Skywalker, ultimately helping bring balance to the Force and the fall of the Empire.
