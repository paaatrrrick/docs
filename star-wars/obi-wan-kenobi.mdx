## Overview

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the most skilled Jedi Masters in galactic history. Known for his wisdom, defensive lightsaber combat, and unwavering dedication to the Jedi Order, he served as a general during the Clone Wars and later became the guardian of Luke Skywalker.

## Notable accomplishments

- Defeated Darth Maul after the fall of his master, Qui-Gon Jinn
- Trained Anakin Skywalker from Padawan to Jedi Knight
- Served as a Clone Wars general alongside Commander Cody
- Survived Order 66 and protected Luke Skywalker on Tatooine
- Sacrificed himself aboard the Death Star to allow the Rebellion to escape

## Legacy

Obi-Wan Kenobi's teachings and sacrifice shaped the future of the galaxy. His guidance helped Luke Skywalker become the Jedi who would ultimately redeem Darth Vader and bring balance to the Force.
