## Overview

Obi-Wan Kenobi is one of the most skilled Jedi Masters in galactic history. Known for his wisdom, patience, and mastery of defensive lightsaber combat, he played a pivotal role in shaping the fate of the galaxy.

## Notable achievements

- Defeated Darth Maul in combat as a Padawan
- Served as a General during the Clone Wars
- Trained both Anakin and Luke Skywalker
- Mastered Form III (Soresu) lightsaber combat

## Legacy

Obi-Wan's teachings and sacrifice helped Luke Skywalker become the Jedi who would ultimately redeem Darth Vader and bring balance to the Force. His influence extends far beyond his physical lifetime, continuing to guide the next generation as a Force ghost.
