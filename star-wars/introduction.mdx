## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas. It depicts the adventures of characters "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away" and has become one of the most influential and successful media franchises in history.

## The saga

The Star Wars saga spans multiple trilogies:

| Trilogy | Films | Era |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Prequel | Episodes I-III | Rise of the Empire |
| Original | Episodes IV-VI | Galactic Civil War |
| Sequel | Episodes VII-IX | First Order conflict |

## Core themes

- **The Force** — An energy field that binds the galaxy together
- **Good vs. Evil** — The eternal struggle between light and dark
- **Redemption** — The possibility of salvation even for the fallen
- **Hope** — The power of belief in impossible circumstances

## Notable characters

- **Luke Skywalker** — The hero who brought balance to the Force
- **Darth Vader** — The fallen Jedi redeemed by his son
- **Princess Leia** — Leader of the Rebel Alliance
- **Han Solo** — Smuggler turned hero
- **Yoda** — Legendary Jedi Master
