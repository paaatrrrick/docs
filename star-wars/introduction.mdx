## Overview

Star Wars is a multimedia franchise created by George Lucas that began with the 1977 film. Set in a galaxy far, far away, it follows the eternal struggle between the light and dark sides of the Force.

## The Skywalker Saga

The main film series consists of three trilogies:

| Trilogy | Films | Era |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Original | Episodes IV-VI | Rebellion vs Empire |
| Prequel | Episodes I-III | Fall of the Republic |
| Sequel | Episodes VII-IX | First Order vs Resistance |
