## Overview

Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi to ever live. Serving as Grand Master of the Jedi Order, he trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a crucial role in the Clone Wars and the eventual redemption of the galaxy.

## Wisdom and teachings

Yoda's philosophical teachings form the foundation of Jedi wisdom:

- **Fear leads to suffering** — "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger, anger leads to hate, hate leads to suffering."
- **Do or do not** — "Do. Or do not. There is no try."
- **Luminous beings** — "Luminous beings are we, not this crude matter."
- **Letting go** — "Train yourself to let go of everything you fear to lose."

## Legacy

Yoda's influence extends far beyond his physical lifetime. His teachings guided Luke Skywalker to become a Jedi Knight and continued to inspire future generations. Even after death, Yoda appeared as a Force ghost to offer guidance, proving that true wisdom transcends mortality.
