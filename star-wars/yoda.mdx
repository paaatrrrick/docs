## Overview

Yoda is one of the most powerful and wise Jedi Masters in the history of the galaxy. For over 800 years, he trained Jedi and served on the Jedi Council, becoming the Grand Master of the Jedi Order.

## Legacy

Yoda's influence on the Jedi Order cannot be overstated. Even after his death on Dagobah, he continued to guide Luke Skywalker as a Force ghost, helping to ensure the eventual defeat of the Empire and the rebirth of the Jedi.
