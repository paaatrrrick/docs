## Overview

Yoda is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. A legendary Jedi Grand Master, he trained Jedi for over 800 years and played a pivotal role in the galaxy's history.

## Key characteristics

- **Species**: Unknown (referred to simply as "Yoda's species")
- **Height**: 66 cm (2'2")
- **Homeworld**: Unknown
- **Affiliation**: Jedi Order, Galactic Republic

## Notable abilities

Yoda was renowned for his exceptional Force abilities:

- **Lightsaber combat**: Despite his small stature, Yoda was one of the greatest duelists in Jedi history
- **Force mastery**: Telekinesis, Force visions, and deep connection to the living Force
- **Wisdom**: Centuries of experience made him the wisest of the Jedi

## Famous quotes

> "Do or do not. There is no try."

> "Fear is the path to the dark side. Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."

> "Luminous beings are we, not this crude matter."

## Legacy

Yoda's teachings and philosophy continue to influence Jedi and Force users throughout the galaxy. His emphasis on patience, wisdom, and the dangers of the dark side remain foundational to Jedi training.
