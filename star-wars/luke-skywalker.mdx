## Overview

Luke Skywalker is the hero at the heart of the original Star Wars trilogy. A humble farm boy from Tatooine, he discovered his true destiny as a Jedi and became the catalyst for the fall of the Galactic Empire and the redemption of Darth Vader.

## Journey to becoming a Jedi

Luke's path from farm boy to Jedi Master:

1. **Discovery** — Found Princess Leia's message in R2-D2
2. **Training begins** — Met Obi-Wan Kenobi and learned of his heritage
3. **Rebel hero** — Destroyed the first Death Star
4. **Yoda's teachings** — Trained on Dagobah with the legendary Master
5. **Confrontation** — Faced Vader and learned the terrible truth
6. **Redemption** — Refused the dark side and saved his father

## Legacy

Luke Skywalker's unwavering belief in redemption changed the course of galactic history. By refusing to kill his father, he broke the cycle of hatred and brought about the fall of the Empire. He later worked to rebuild the Jedi Order, passing on what he had learned to a new generation.
