## Overview

Luke Skywalker is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. Born on Tatooine, he grew from a humble farm boy to become one of the greatest Jedi Knights in galactic history.

## Key facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Homeworld | Tatooine |
| Species | Human |
| Affiliation | Rebel Alliance, New Jedi Order |
| Master | Obi-Wan Kenobi, Yoda |
| Lightsaber | Blue (later green) |

## Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early life">
    Raised by his uncle Owen and aunt Beru on a moisture farm on Tatooine, unaware of his true heritage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discovery">
    Purchased droids R2-D2 and C-3PO, leading to his meeting with Obi-Wan Kenobi and learning about the Force.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rebellion">
    Joined the Rebel Alliance and destroyed the first Death Star during the Battle of Yavin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Training">
    Trained under Yoda on Dagobah to become a Jedi Knight.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redemption">
    Confronted Darth Vader and Emperor Palpatine, ultimately redeeming his father Anakin Skywalker.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notable quotes

<Note>
"I am a Jedi, like my father before me."
</Note>

## Legacy

Luke Skywalker's journey from farm boy to Jedi Master represents the classic hero's journey. His unwavering belief in the good within his father, Darth Vader, ultimately led to the fall of the Empire and the restoration of balance to the Force.
