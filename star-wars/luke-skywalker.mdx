## Overview

Luke Skywalker is the central hero of the original Star Wars trilogy. A humble farm boy from Tatooine, he rose to become the greatest Jedi of his generation and the one who redeemed his father, Darth Vader.

## Journey to becoming a Jedi

Luke's path from farm boy to Jedi Master followed a classic hero's journey:

- **The call** — Discovered Leia's message in R2-D2
- **The mentor** — Trained under Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda
- **The trials** — Faced Darth Vader and the Emperor
- **The triumph** — Redeemed his father and saved the galaxy

## Legacy

Luke Skywalker's greatest achievement was not destroying the Death Star or defeating the Emperor—it was seeing the good in his father when no one else could. His faith in redemption saved Anakin Skywalker and brought balance to the Force, proving that compassion is the true strength of a Jedi.
