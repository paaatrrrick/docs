## Overview

Luke Skywalker is the central hero of the original Star Wars trilogy. A farm boy from Tatooine, he rose to become the last Jedi Knight and the one who ultimately redeemed his father, Darth Vader, bringing balance to the Force.

## Key facts

| Attribute | Value |

| Homeworld | Tatooine |

| Lightsaber color | Blue (later green) |
| Master | Obi-Wan Kenobi, Yoda |
| Father | Anakin Skywalker (Darth Vader) |

## Journey to becoming a Jedi

Luke's path from farm boy to Jedi Master:

- **A New Hope** — Rescued Princess Leia and destroyed the first Death Star
- **The Empire Strikes Back** — Trained with Yoda and confronted Vader
- **Return of the Jedi** — Redeemed his father and defeated the Empire

## Legacy

Luke Skywalker represents the triumph of hope over despair. By refusing to give up on his father despite everyone telling him Vader was beyond saving, he proved that redemption is always possible. His legacy lives on through the Jedi he trained and the hope he inspired across the galaxy.
