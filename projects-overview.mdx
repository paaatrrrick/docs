## Chemistry research

An instructor facilitation coding scheme visualization tool for chemistry education research.

**Live site:** [facilitation-visualization.netlify.app](https://facilitation-visualization.netlify.app/)

### What it does

- Converts Excel data files into visual timeline representations
- Renders various shapes (rectangles, circles, triangles, diamonds, hexagons) to represent different teaching behaviors
- Categorizes instructor actions including:
  - **Managing** - Classroom management, directions, encouragement, collaboration
  - **Questioning** - Content questions, rhetorical questions, cold calls
  - **Relaying** - Answering student questions, explaining answers, giving hints
  - **Interaction types** - Group interactions, whole class interruptions
